We offer all customers a 100% Freshness Guarantee.
We strive to always deliver quality product. However, we know that by nature flowers are sometimes unpredictable and it is possible that the flowers will not be perfect all the time. At Bouquet Bar we want to make sure you’re happy with your Bouquet Bar experience. This means we will provide an equivalent store credit or send out a replacement item to you at no additional cost when appropriate.

our mission

We’re bringing gifting back.

Gifting is an art, and here at Bouquet Bar, we’re making sure that art is alive and well. A gift is an expression of love, gratitude and friendship.  Bouquet Bar cherishes the old-world tradition of giving with all the modern convenience you’ve come to expect and love.


So join us, and celebrate the art of gifting.
